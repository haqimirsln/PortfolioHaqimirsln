About Me

Hi there! I'm Izzat Haqimi, a passionate Software Engineer and recent graduate from Universiti Malaysia Terengganu. I specialize in building scalable web applications that merge technology with sustainability and social impact.

At SEATRU's Digital Team, I've developed conservation management systems that reduced data processing time from 3 hours to 5 minutes, enabling researchers to focus on protecting endangered sea turtles. This experience taught me that great code isn't just about performance, it's about solving real problems for real people.

My approach combines clean code, accessibility-first design, and user empathy. Whether optimizing database queries, designing intuitive interfaces, or implementing RESTful APIs, I focus on creating experiences that work seamlessly for everyone.
